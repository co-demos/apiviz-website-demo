This is a work in progress to identify various tools oriented both web and data, whether they are free or not. This benchmark aims to enable everyone to identify, according to their own needs, which tools would be best suited to their project.

The Apiviz / Solidata project was born from the impression that this ecosystem of tools of the open source bricks of a data visualization CMS (Content Management System) and a tool of "API-fication" of data was lacking.

You can check this spreadsheet at this address, downnload it in .pdf or download it in .csv. Feel free to point out tools that seem interesting to you by posting on Twitter @jparis_py .
