Valorize your data
with the Apiviz / Solidata system


Valorizing your data is a digital challenge by itself

Your core business is to produce data, that is, industry-specific data sets, and you want a website where you can explore your data, show the services they can offer underneath, so in short show your data in their best light.

However, we often realize that there is a gap between having the data available and the project of making its data intelligible to the general public. In fact, the technologies used to build or enhance / visualize data are different, as is the approach, the expectations of the public or the design.

Why Apiviz ?

The Apiviz / Solidata system was developed as part of a public project of general interest, Entrepreneur d'Intérêt Général , with the aim that the general public (or at least your target public) can access and explore datasets opened by public or parapublic structures.

The system has also been developed to make it possible for any type of organization to benefit from such a data infrastructure in a short time and at a low cost: be it man-time or cost in material.

The advantages of the Apiviz system

The Apiviz / Solidata system has several advantages when you want to allow your audience to explore your data:

  • a customizable website (UI, contents, data)
  • online data management
  • expose data to third-party services (data API-fication)
  • data protection and publishing rights management
  • a modular system (independent microservices)
  • open source codes (documentation here)
  • mutualised maintainance and R&D (SAAS / PAAS)
